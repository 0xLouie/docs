## Available Endpoints

### Public Endpoints
- `/markets` - List all markets
- `/markets/{id}` - Market details
- `/markets/{id}/orderbook` - Order book
- `/markets/{id}/trades` - Recent trades
- `/markets/{id}/history` - Price history

### Private Endpoints
- `/orders` - Place/cancel orders
- `/positions` - View positions
- `/balance` - Account balance
- `/history` - Trade history
- `/referrals` - Referral stats
