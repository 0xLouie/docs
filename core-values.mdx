## Design Principles

### 1. Speed Without Compromise
Leveraging Solana's sub-second finality while maintaining security through rigorous smart contract audits.

### 2. Simplicity in Complexity
Complex market mechanics simplified into intuitive user experiences that anyone can understand.

### 3. Incentive Alignment
Every participant benefits from platform growth through the $PREDIQT flywheel mechanism.

### 4. Resilience by Design
No single point of failure. Distributed oracles, decentralized governance, and immutable smart contracts.
