## Step-by-Step Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install a Wallet" icon="1">
    ### Choose and Install a Solana Wallet
    
    We recommend [Phantom](https://phantom.app) for beginners:
    
    1. Visit phantom.app
    2. Click "Download" for your browser
    3. Follow the installation prompts
    4. **Important:** Save your recovery phrase securely!
    
    <Warning>
      Never share your recovery phrase with anyone. Write it down and store it safely offline.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fund Your Wallet" icon="2">
    ### Add SOL to Your Wallet
    
    **Option A: Buy directly in wallet**
    - Click "Buy" in Phantom
    - Use MoonPay or other providers
    - Purchase at least 0.1 SOL
    
    **Option B: Transfer from exchange**
    - Buy SOL on Coinbase, Binance, etc.
    - Copy your Phantom wallet address
    - Withdraw SOL to your wallet
    
    <Tip>
      Keep a small amount of SOL (~0.01) for transaction fees, even if betting with $PREDIQT.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect to Prediqtmarket" icon="3">
    ### Access the Platform
    
    1. Visit [prediqt.market](https://prediqt.market)
    2. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
    3. Select your wallet (Phantom)
    4. Approve the connection request
    
    You'll see your wallet balance displayed once connected.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore Markets" icon="4">
    ### Browse Available Predictions
    
    Markets are organized by category:
    - **Trending** - Most active markets
    - **Politics** - Elections, policies
    - **Crypto** - Token prices, launches
    - **Sports** - Games, championships
    - **News** - Current events
    
    Click any market to view details and current odds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Market Information

When you open a market, you'll see:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Market Details">
    - **Question**: What you're betting on
    - **Resolution Date**: When the market closes
    - **Oracle Source**: How the outcome is determined
    - **Total Volume**: Amount bet so far
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Current Odds">
    - **YES Price**: Cost per YES share (probability)
    - **NO Price**: Cost per NO share
    - **Your Position**: Any shares you own
    - **Order Book**: Recent trades
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Market Info">
    - **Resolution Criteria**: Exact conditions for YES/NO
    - **Market Creator**: Who created the market
    - **Liquidity**: Available for trading
    - **Fee Structure**: Trading fees
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Your First Bet

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="1. Choose Your Market" icon="search">
    Start with a simple, short-term market you understand well. Sports games or upcoming announcements are good for beginners.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="2. Analyze the Odds" icon="chart-line">
    If YES shares cost $0.70, the market thinks there's a 70% chance of that outcome. Do you agree or disagree?
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="3. Decide Your Position" icon="scale-balanced">
    - Think it will happen? Buy YES shares
    - Think it won't? Buy NO shares
    - Remember: Winning shares pay $1.00
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="4. Enter Your Amount" icon="dollar-sign">
    Start small! Try betting 0.1 SOL (~$5-10) for your first prediction to understand the mechanics.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="5. Confirm Transaction" icon="check">
    Review the details and approve in your wallet. Your shares will appear in your portfolio immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pro Tips for Beginners

<Tip>
  1. **Start Small**: Begin with markets you understand and small amounts
  2. **Research First**: Check resolution criteria carefully before betting
  3. **Diversify**: Don't put all funds in one market
  4. **Set Limits**: Decide your maximum loss before betting
  5. **Learn from Others**: Join our Discord to discuss strategies
</Tip>
